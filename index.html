About NeuroAmateurs

NeuroAmateurs was created to make neuroscience accessible to everyone. Whether you're a student, professional in another field, or simply curious about how the brain works, our mission is to provide clear, engaging, and scientifically accurate content.

Our team consists of neuroscientists, educators, and science communicators who are passionate about sharing the wonders of the brain with the world.
